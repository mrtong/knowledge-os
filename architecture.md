# Knowledge OS

## Vision

Knowledge belongs to humans, not platforms.

This project is designed to ensure that personal knowledge remains portable,
versioned, AI-readable and future-proof.

Notion is an editor.

Markdown is the storage format.

Git preserves history.

AI creates understanding.

---

## Goals

- Own my knowledge forever
- Avoid vendor lock-in
- Make every note AI-readable
- Keep complete history of thinking
- Support multiple AI models
- Build a true Second Brain

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Markdown First

Every piece of knowledge must eventually become Markdown.

Markdown is the canonical representation.

---

### 2. Git is Source of Truth

Git stores history.

Notion does not.

NotebookLM does not.

AI does not.

---

### 3. AI Native

The repository is designed for AI.

Every document should be understandable by:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- NotebookLM
- Future models

---

### 4. Platform Independent

Editors come and go.

Knowledge stays.

Supported editors may include:

- Notion
- Obsidian
- VSCode
- Apple Notes (future)
- Logseq (future)

---

### 5. Knowledge Evolves

Ideas evolve.

The system should preserve the evolution of ideas,
not only their latest version.
